STAAD Pro

STAAD Pro empowers me to perform complex structural analysis and design with confidence. I use it to analyze various loads (dead load, live load, wind, seismic) acting on structures and to design components that can withstand those forces safely. My work includes modeling multi-story buildings, bridges, and industrial frames, ensuring compliance with IS, Euro, and ACI codes. I leverage STAAD's visualization tools and design reports to optimize material use, reduce costs, and enhance structural integrity. This software helps me validate designs before implementation, preventing failures and streamlining approvals from engineering authorities.

Surveying

Surveying forms the basis of accurate site planning and design. I possess hands-on experience with Total Station, GPS, Theodolite, and Auto Level instruments to conduct various types of surveys including topographical, boundary, leveling, and contour mapping. I ensure the accurate transfer of design data from paper to the ground by performing layout marking and elevation checks. My work supports essential tasks like calculating earthwork quantities, preparing site maps, and validating land titles. Precision in surveying not only helps in legal compliance but also plays a crucial role in minimizing design and execution discrepancies during construction.
